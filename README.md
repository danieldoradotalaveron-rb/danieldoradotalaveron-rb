### What I work on

Most of my day-to-day is **applied AI for operations**: designing agent workflows, RAG and GraphRAG pipelines, evaluation when ground truth is scarce, and wiring agents to real data through **MCP servers** and event-driven ingestion.

I like the full path from research-ish ideas to something deployable — tracing, metrics, chaos-style hardening on **Kubernetes**, and CI/CD that keeps models and agents honest.

On the side I tinker with **robotics** (ROS 2, FK/IK, RViz) and still carry a soft spot for **3D / technical art** from earlier days.
